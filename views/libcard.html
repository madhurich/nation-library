Services offered

Audio books

If you have a long commute time to work, an upcoming road trip, or otherwise just spend a lot of time in the car, you can get books on tape from the Nation library. It’s a good deal if you usually pay for audio books on iTunes, and it might make your morning commute a little more exciting. Nation Libray, even offers audio books that are compatible with your iPod..

Local Art Work

Nation library has a monthly artist showcase. The director chooses an artist each month to display their best work. Each piece is priced to sell, and it’s great for local artists looking for extra support and publicity. Nation library can be a great place to buy contemporary fine art on a budget..

Personal development resources

Did you know that Nation library offer college prep services and career support? for example, Nation Libray invites college-bound high school students to brush up on their test-taking skills with a free practice exam. We even offer business seminars and workshops from local Human Resources professionals to help people get a head start on their new job hunt or guide college students to find internships and summer jobs..
